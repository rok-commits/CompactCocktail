Good to know

Frequently asked.

Do I need my own tap system?

No special equipment — CoCo pours through a classic beer tap system. All you need is an “F” type KeyKeg coupler and standard cleaning gear, which most venues already have. No tap on site? A mobile unit solves it, and we sort the setup together.

What keg sizes do you offer?

12 L PolyKegs and 30 L kegs. A 12 L PolyKeg pours about 72–75 servings — each one 160 ml, poured exactly as a bartender would make it, dilution included.

How long does a keg keep once it’s tapped?

2–3 weeks once opened. The PolyKeg’s bag-in-keg design means gas only ever squeezes the bag from outside — it never touches the cocktail, so nothing oxidizes. Unopened, a keg keeps for at least 12 months from its production date.

What expertise does my staff need?

None — it works exactly like pouring a beer. A cocktail is ready in 10–12 seconds, versus 2–3 minutes for a hand-made one, and the alcohol content (8–10% ABV) matches the traditional recipe exactly.

How is it delivered and set up?

Free delivery within Budapest for orders of at least one keg; countrywide delivery is arranged with partner carriers. You can also pick kegs up in person at our Soroksári út location. We set the system up with you the first time and leave you with ongoing support and a troubleshooting manual.

What does it cost?

12 L kegs run roughly 57,000–67,000 Ft depending on flavour (about 690–890 Ft per drink), 30 L from ~130,000 Ft. A 2,000 Ft admin fee applies per order, and prices exclude VAT. Full per-flavour and volume pricing is in the price list above — or get in touch and we’ll walk you through the numbers.
